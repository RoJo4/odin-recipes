Low Tyramine Smoothie

Recipe Description

This smoothie contains fruits and dairy and is suitable for those on a low-tyramine diet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 cup of Strawberries
  • 1 Banana

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients and place in blender
  2. Blend for 2 mins or according to your blenders settings
  3. Pour into glass and decorate with a sliced strawberry on top!
