Low Tyramine Broccolini

Recipe Description

A nutritious simple recipe requiring less than 10 ingredients. Sauteed Broccolini with garlic and red pepper flakes
Recipe from kitchin

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches of Broccolini
  • 1 tablespon of olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Lemon wedges to serve

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients and place in blender
  2. Blend for 2 mins or according to your blenders settings
  3. Pour into glass and decorate with a sliced strawberry on top!
