Easy Broccolini

Recipe Description

A nutritious simple recipe requiring less than 10 ingredients. Sauteed Broccolini with garlic and red pepper flakes
Recipe from kitchin

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches of Broccolini
  • 1 tablespon of olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • Lemon wedges to serve
  • Optional: Slivered almond flakes

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients: wash broccolini and trim 1/2 inch off the bottom stems
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until the oil is hot
  3. Add the Broccolini and saute until its bright green and some of the stems and florets are lightly charred, approx 5-7 minutes
  4. Add the garlic, salt, red pepper and almond flakes. Continue to sautes until the garlic is fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  5. Add the water, cover, and cook until the Broccolini is vibrant green and crisp-tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve immediately, with lemod wedges, if using
